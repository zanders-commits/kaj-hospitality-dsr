Section 02  /  Business Case + Proposed Solution

The Problem You're Solving — and How Reeco Closes It.

Four things slowing your team down today — and how Reeco closes each one.

01
The Problem

BirchStreet's Punch-Out Model Slows Your Team Down

BirchStreet sends your team out to each supplier's website to order, one vendor at a time. For your operators this creates extra steps without clear benefits — which is likely where adoption breaks down and off-system buying creeps back in. It also makes it very difficult to control which products your team orders from each supplier, so GPO compliance and brand standards are hard to enforce.

The Answer

Every Supplier in One Marketplace

Reeco brings all your suppliers into one curated marketplace — making it simple to create multiple POs at once and see all the options you want your properties buying, across every supplier, in one place. Your HSM (or any GPO) agreements are built right in, so the on-program, best choice is easy to identify. It behaves like a consumer shopping experience, so the learning curve for GMs and executive housekeepers is hours, not days — and adoption sticks.

02
The Problem

Ottimate Is a Standalone AP Tool

Ottimate handles AP as a separate point solution with a premium, per-invoice cost that can get expensive fast. It isn't connected to how your properties buy, so coding is done twice and may not be consistent between the PO and the invoice. There's a delay sending data to M3, the integration isn't as robust, and M3 charges integration fees — fees that are waived through Reeco's strategic partnership.

The Answer

Invoices Coded in Seconds, Synced Real-Time with M3

Reeco captures every invoice and applies the correct GL code from your M3 chart of accounts, learning as it goes. Approved invoices sync to M3 in real time through a direct API — with advanced integration features available only to Reeco customers — alongside ProfitSword budget data and a clear path to one-click vendor payments through the Reeco, M3, and Corpay partnership. One fixed cost covers unlimited invoices instead of a fee for each one.

03
The Problem

Receiving Is Manual and Disconnected From the Order

Receiving is disconnected from the PO platform and the invoice. That means missing items, short shipments, and credits can slip through the cracks — so what you pay for may not always be what actually showed up at the property.

The Answer

Mobile Receiving, Backed by Reeco's Team

Your team checks in orders on the spot from the mobile app, right against the PO. Reeco's support and receiving services handle missing items and credits for you — recovering every credit owed — and you only pay for what actually arrived, with the PO and invoice compared so you're paying the right amount.

04
The Problem

Purchasing, Receiving, and AP Live in Separate Systems

Purchasing, receiving, and AP each live in a different place, so nothing ties a PO to what was received to the invoice that follows. Reconciling across systems is manual, and no single platform shows the full picture from order to payment.

The Answer

One Platform, From PO to Payment

Reeco brings purchasing, receiving, and AP into one connected platform, so every PO, receipt, and invoice line up automatically — and it's integrated with HSM, M3, and ProfitSword. You get a single source of truth from order to payment, with one login and one support team behind it.

Section 08  /  FAQs

Questions We Expect — Answered Directly.

If something's missing, email Bill and we'll add it before the meeting.

"We're also looking at Food Buy's OneSource. How is Reeco different?"
OneSource is the same punch-out model that drove your BirchStreet adoption issues — your team leaves to order on each supplier's own site, one vendor at a time. Reeco is a single consolidated marketplace across all your suppliers, with a consumer-style mobile experience. That difference — the system actually being easy to use — is what gets GMs and department heads to adopt it, which is where the savings and control come from.
"Can we keep our current GPO and supplier agreements?"
Yes — that's entirely your call. Reeco isn't a GPO and doesn't change who you buy through. We work with the program you choose: your existing supplier agreements and pricing are honored, and through our data-sharing agreement with HSM we tag your on-program items in the store and help you track participation. You keep the relationship; Reeco just makes buying on-program easier.
"How does Reeco replace Ottimate for AP?"
Reeco AP is a fixed cost for unlimited invoices — no per-invoice fee. Every invoice is coded against your M3 chart of accounts and matched to the order and what was received, so you pay the right amount, only for what arrived. As an M3 strategic partner, Reeco syncs in real time with integration fees waived, and your AP setup is waived too.
"How long does onboarding take?"
AP can be live within about a week, and both modules — Purchasing & Receiving and AP Automation — are typically live within 2–3 weeks. A dedicated customer success partner from Reeco guides the rollout, so it stays a light lift on your side.
